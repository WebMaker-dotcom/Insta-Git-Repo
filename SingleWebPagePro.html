Our story

Built by a team who felt the pain.

"We were drowning in tools. Lumis was our way out — and we built it for everyone."

In 2021, three founders were running a product team across six different applications. Meetings were replicated in Slack, tasks scattered across Jira, docs in Notion — with nothing actually connected. Lumis was born from that frustration.

Today, over 50,000 teams use Lumis to bring their work together. We're a remote-first company with people across 18 countries, united by a shared belief: great tools should feel effortless.
